# Getting Started with Steno

## What is Steno?

Steno is an AI-powered demand letter generator designed specifically for law firms. It streamlines the process of creating professional, comprehensive demand letters by combining your case documents with intelligent AI assistance and customizable templates.

### Key Benefits

- **Save Time**: Generate complete demand letters in minutes, not hours
- **Maintain Quality**: AI ensures consistency and professional tone
- **Collaborate Seamlessly**: Work together in real-time with your team
- **Stay Organized**: Track versions, comments, and export history
- **Work Efficiently**: Refine specific sections with AI assistance

## Your First Demand Letter

Follow these steps to create your first demand letter from start to finish:

### Step 1: Create a Project

1. From the **Projects Dashboard**, click **New Project**
2. Fill in the project details:
   - **Project Title**: Brief description of the case (e.g., "Smith v. Johnson - Car Accident")
   - **Client Name**: Your client's full name
   - **Case Type**: Select from dropdown (Personal Injury, Contract Dispute, etc.)
3. Click **Create Project**

[Screenshot: New Project Form]

### Step 2: Upload Source Documents

Upload evidence and supporting documents that will inform the AI:

1. Click **Upload Documents** or drag files into the upload area
2. Supported formats:
   - PDF files (police reports, medical records, correspondence)
   - Word documents (.docx)
   - Images (.jpg, .png) - OCR will extract text
3. Wait for processing (green checkmark indicates completion)
4. Review extracted text by clicking on any document

**Best Practices:**
- Upload clear, readable documents
- Include all relevant evidence (medical records, photos, correspondence)
- Larger files (>50MB) may take longer to process

[Screenshot: Document Upload Interface]

### Step 3: Select a Template

1. Click **Select Template**
2. Browse available templates:
   - **Personal Injury Demand Letter** - For accident and injury cases
   - **Contract Breach Demand** - For contract disputes
   - **Property Damage Demand** - For property-related claims
   - **Custom Templates** - Firm-specific templates
3. Click **Use This Template**

[Screenshot: Template Gallery]

### Step 4: Generate AI Draft

1. Review case details and make any corrections
2. Click **Generate Draft**
3. Watch as the AI streams content in real-time (typically 30-60 seconds)
4. The editor will display the generated demand letter with proper formatting

**What the AI Does:**
- Analyzes your source documents
- Extracts key facts, dates, and amounts
- Applies legal reasoning based on the template
- Generates professional, persuasive language
- Structures content according to the template sections

[Screenshot: AI Generation Progress]

### Step 5: Review and Edit

1. Read through the generated draft carefully
2. Make manual edits:
   - Click anywhere in the text to edit
   - Use the toolbar for formatting (bold, italic, headings, lists)
   - Add or remove sections as needed
3. Verify all facts against your source documents
4. Check names, dates, and amounts for accuracy

**Important:** Always verify AI-generated content. The AI is a powerful assistant, but human review is essential for accuracy and legal compliance.

### Step 6: Refine with AI (Optional)

Improve specific sections using AI refinement:

1. Select text you want to improve
2. Right-click and choose **Refine with AI**
3. Choose a Quick Action:
   - **Make More Assertive** - Strengthen demands
   - **Add More Detail** - Expand with context
   - **Shorten This Section** - Condense content
   - **Emphasize Liability** - Highlight defendant's fault
   - **Soften Tone** - Make more conciliatory
   - **Improve Clarity** - Simplify complex language
4. Or write custom instructions in the "Custom Instructions" tab
5. Click **Apply Refinement**
6. Review the refined version and accept or discard changes

See the [AI Refinement Guide](./ai-refinement.md) for detailed instructions.

[Screenshot: Refinement Panel]

### Step 7: Export to Word

1. Click the **Export** button in the top toolbar
2. Configure export options:
   - **Include Metadata**: Add version and timestamp to footer
   - **Include Letterhead**: Add your firm's logo and contact info
3. Click **Export to Word**
4. Your browser will download a .docx file
5. Open in Microsoft Word or Google Docs for final review

**Export Features:**
- Professional letterhead with firm logo
- Page numbers and metadata
- Proper legal formatting (margins, fonts, spacing)
- Version tracking

[Screenshot: Export Modal]

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does AI generation take?**
A: Typically 30-60 seconds for a complete demand letter, depending on document length and complexity.

**Q: Can I edit while my colleague is also editing?**
A: Yes! Steno uses real-time synchronization. You'll see each other's changes instantly.

**Q: What happens if I lose internet connection?**
A: Your recent changes are cached locally. When reconnected, they'll sync automatically.

**Q: Can I use Steno on mobile devices?**
A: The editor works best on desktop or tablet. Mobile viewing is supported, but editing is limited.

**Q: How secure is my data?**
A: All documents are encrypted at rest (S3 SSE-S3) and in transit (HTTPS). Data is isolated by firm - you can only access your firm's documents.

**Q: Can I delete a project?**
A: Yes, from the Projects Dashboard, click the three-dot menu and select "Delete". Note: This cannot be undone.
